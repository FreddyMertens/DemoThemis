Rules underneath every state

These are guardrails and control planes, not extra lifecycle steps.

Custody and proofFunds lock before an unknown panel exists. One bound random draw, live presence checks, private ballots, and an aggregate tally protect each disputed case.
Two governmentsThe market token may tune business settings, including a 0–1% margin above the court invoice. Person-weighted court governance may tune court settings. Neither is the judge in an active case.
Constitutional firewallThe court invoice routes first, the market token cannot appoint or pay the bench, final money never reopens, and later collusion reports punish people rather than rewrite payment.
