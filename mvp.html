Evidence, not theatre

Three defenses you can trace today

Each claim is tied to a working mechanism, from identity check to final settlement.

Read scope notes
Identity defense

One human, one seat

  • Source-verified World Chain contracts
  • World ID 4.0 verifier inside registration
  • Duplicate-human nullifier rejection
Panel defense

A new draw for every case

  • Cases draw jurors after they open
  • One seat per registered human
  • Commit/reveal binds each ballot
  • No-shows trigger redraw or safe fallback
Settlement defense

The ruling moves the money

  • Resolve counts revealed votes by rule
  • Escrow releases or refunds by verdict
  • Fees route 70 / 20 / 10 atomically
  • Public events leave an audit trail
