From proposal to software

Four important locks you can trace

1

One person

World ID gates juror registration and a spent nullifier stops the same human returning through another wallet.

2

One panel

The court selects a small demo jury after the dispute exists, rather than letting the richest accounts appoint themselves.

3

One sealed round

Jurors commit a hidden vote first and reveal later, so early votes cannot steer the rest of the panel.

4

One settlement

The final result routes escrow and fees atomically, keeping the court decision tied to the money movement.

Current scope

A real MVP, with honest edges.

The sandbox is intentionally simulated. The court dashboard reads public chain data. World App is required for supported identity and transaction flows. Test tokens have no value, the “open a dispute” form is currently a disabled preview, and the three-human capstone remains pending.
