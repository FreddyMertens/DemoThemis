Rules underneath every state

Three permanent rails keep every route neutral and fully collateralized.

Custody and proofFunds lock before an unknown panel exists. One bound random draw, live presence checks, private ballots, and an aggregate tally protect each disputed case.
Independent governanceOmenMarketMaker may tune its own market fee and product settings. DemoThemis may tune its own service pricing and court settings. Neither token reaches across the product boundary or judges an active case.
Commercial boundaryWhen OmenMarketMaker selects DemoThemis, it pays the quoted resolution fee as a client. Fees, treasuries, and token economics otherwise remain separate; final money never reopens.
