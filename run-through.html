Rules underneath every state

Three permanent rails keep every product view neutral and fully collateralized.

Custody and proofFunds lock before an unknown panel exists. One bound random draw, live presence checks, private ballots, and an aggregate tally protect every court case.
Independent governanceOmenMarketMaker may tune its own market fee and product settings. DemoThemis may tune its own service pricing and court settings. Neither token reaches across the product boundary or judges an active case.
Automatic funding boundaryEvery deposit contributes pro-rata to a locked resolution reserve under a target and maximum rate fixed before betting. A funded reserve pays DemoThemis automatically; a short reserve triggers top-up or refund, never an unpaid jury.
