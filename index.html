The products

PredictionMoMo markets, DemoThemis arbitration

Every market ends with a ruling: did the thing happen or not? Polymarket, the biggest prediction market in the world, hands that ruling to a protocol called UMA. Anyone can post a bond and propose an answer. If nobody objects within two hours, it stands. If someone does, UMA's token holders vote on the truth, one token one vote, and voters who side against the majority lose part of their stake. So the rational move is to follow the biggest wallets, and disputed markets have settled against plain reality because of it. Whoever holds enough tokens holds the verdict.

Polymarket also picks which markets exist. You cannot open one yourself, keep one private among friends, or post your position as collateral. The six features below are exactly what is missing, and one change unlocks them all: we swap the token vote for a court of verified humans, one person one vote. That is a huge upgrade over UMA, and it turns a betting site into something bigger: protocol escrow with arbitration built in.

The two products: PredictionMoMo and DemoThemis

Product one

PredictionMoMo: markets anyone can open

  1. Arbitration not gameable by UMA whales: no chance of getting scammed.
  2. Liquid markets tokenize into ERC-20 YES and NO tokens you self-custody; this lets you provide liquidity and earn interest, post them as collateral, and trade them with leverage.
  3. Anyone can open private, invite-only markets and still have a world-class third-party escrow and dispute system: post your side at any odds you name, invite the other side, and the odds stay fixed for everyone who joins.
  4. Start a new prediction market instantly, on any topic, with any liquidity, even with less than a dollar.
  5. With self-custody tokens that stay tradeable during disputes and resolution windows, you can exit the market at any time if there is liquidity. This also means there is no rush to close dispute windows, allowing retroactive changes to resolution windows where necessary.
  6. Native peer-to-peer parlays on liquid markets. By integrating Conditional Tokens Framework (CTF) via token splitting, bettors get live odds and early cashouts without the protocol or a "bookie" ever taking bankruptcy risk.
Product two

DemoThemis: protocol escrow and arbitration

Goal: a protocol escrow and arbitration service so good that customers want every online deal to run through it, from Uber to eBay to Airbnb to AdSense. Apps bring demand and interface; DemoThemis holds the funds, decides disputes, and releases or refunds by rule.

How: robust, ungameable mechanics with proof of personhood to overcome the need for plutocracy. PredictionMoMo supplies the first demand, data, and escrow volume.

Using Worldcoin to ensure 1 juror = 1 human: a World ID iris scan makes each arbitrator one unique person with one vote, a face check on their own phone confirms presence at every draw, and settlement runs on-chain with gas sponsored for verified humans.
