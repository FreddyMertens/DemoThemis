What we are building

DemoThemis arbitration service & OmenMarketMaker prediction market

DemoThemis and OmenMarketMaker are entirely separate products but both require the other to exist, DemoThemis needs OmenMarketMaker to bootstrap its existence and create demand and OmenMarketMaker needs DemoThemis' superior arbitration to create a superior product to existing markets.

Product one Resolution layer

DemoThemis

Arbitration service: the most ambitious human infrastructure ever made.

Goal: a protocol escrow and arbitration service so good that customers want every online deal to run through it, from Uber to eBay to Airbnb to AdSense. Apps bring demand and interface; DemoThemis holds the funds, decides disputes, and releases or refunds by rule.

How: robust, ungameable mechanics with proof of personhood to overcome the need for plutocracy. OmenMarketMaker supplies the first demand, data, and escrow volume.

Using Worldcoin to ensure 1 juror = 1 human: a World ID iris scan makes each arbitrator one unique person with one vote, a face check on their own phone confirms presence at every draw, and settlement runs on-chain with gas sponsored for verified humans.

Product two Prediction market

OmenMarketMaker

Six novel market features, combined to make a superior prediction market platform

  1. Outcomes cannot be bought and arbitration & appeals are robust & reliable: Every funded final outcome comes from independent verified-human arbitration; an underfunded market refunds without declaring a winner.
  2. Start a new prediction market instantly, on any topic, with any liquidity, even with less than a dollar.
  3. Liquid markets become self-custodied ERC-20 YES and NO tokens for liquidity, collateral, and leverage.
  4. Claims remain tradeable through resolution and appeal windows, so holders can exit whenever liquidity exists.
  5. Bookmaker-style parlays, cashouts, and leveraged YES/NO token trading, with every payout fully backed.
  6. Private invite-only markets offer fixed odds with third-party escrow and neutral resolution.
